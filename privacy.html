Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Muscle Slime ("the app", "we") is a personal training tracker. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and who it is shared with. The app does not require an account or login.

1. Data We Collect

  • Device identifier — a randomly generated device_id stored on your device. It is not linked to your name, email, Apple ID, or any real-world identity.
  • Training records — workouts, exercises, sets, weights, and repetitions you enter.
  • Body metrics — body weight, body fat percentage, and muscle mass you choose to record.
  • Usage analytics — anonymous in-app events (for example, completing a workout or viewing your slime) collected via PostHog to help us improve the app.
  • Crash diagnostics — technical crash reports collected via Firebase Crashlytics when the app stops unexpectedly.

We do not collect your name, email address, contacts, precise location, photos, or health data from Apple Health.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To store your training and body records and show your progress.
  • To grow your in-app character based on your activity.
  • To understand aggregate usage and improve the app.
  • To diagnose and fix crashes.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

3. Where Your Data Is Stored

Your records are stored locally on your device and backed up to our cloud database so they are not lost. Data is associated only with the anonymous device_id.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following providers, each with its own privacy policy:

  • Supabase — cloud database backup of your records.
  • PostHog — anonymous product analytics.
  • RevenueCat — processing of in-app purchases.
  • Firebase Crashlytics (Google) — crash reporting.
  • Apple App Store — purchase transactions.

5. In-App Purchases

The app offers optional cosmetic items (such as hats and backgrounds). Purchases are processed by Apple and RevenueCat. We never receive your payment card details.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is kept while the app is installed. Deleting the app removes the local data on your device. To request deletion of cloud-stored data associated with your device, contact us at the address below.

7. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

For privacy questions or data deletion requests, contact: komon.app@gmail.com
